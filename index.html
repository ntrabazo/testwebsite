About Me

I'm a Cybersecurity student at Kennesaw State University pursuing concurrent B.S. and M.S. degrees through the Double Owl accelerated pathway — already enrolled in graduate-level coursework with a 3.61 GPA. My foundation is in GRC methodology and technical security tools: Linux, Splunk, Wireshark, and risk frameworks.

As President of Theta Chi — the largest IFC chapter on campus — I lead strategic direction and daily operations, represent the chapter to university leadership and the national organization, and manage the executive board overseeing all committees and initiatives. I'm also a member of Order of Omega, selected among the top 3% of Greek-letter organization members at KSU for academic achievement, leadership, and service.

I'm part of the next generation of security professionals who see AI not as a threat to security, but as its most powerful tool. I'm obsessed with efficiency — in systems, in workflows, in every process I touch — and I'm actively building the skills to operate at the intersection of both fields.
